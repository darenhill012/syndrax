Next-Generation Infrastructure Observability

GridWatch is an advanced monitoring and observability platform built for modern cloud-native infrastructure. It provides real-time visibility into your entire technology stack, from containers and microservices to databases and network components.

Designed for DevOps teams, SREs, and IT operations professionals, GridWatch combines powerful metrics collection, intelligent alerting, and intuitive dashboards to help you maintain peak system performance and reliability.

Key Features

Comprehensive monitoring capabilities for enterprise infrastructure.

Real-Time Metrics

Sub-second metric collection with automatic discovery of services, containers, and cloud resources across your infrastructure.

Intelligent Alerting

ML-powered anomaly detection and smart alert routing to reduce noise and surface critical issues before they impact users.
