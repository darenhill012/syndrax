Syndrax Sync was created by sellers who were frustrated by the tools that existed — so we built the platform we always needed.

The Problem Nobody Had Solved

Every e-commerce seller we talked to had the same problem: too many platforms, too little time, and zero visibility into what was actually making money. You'd update Amazon, forget eBay, oversell on Shopify, and spend your weekends manually reconciling spreadsheets that were already outdated.

Existing tools either did too little or required a developer to set up. Enterprise solutions were built for enterprise budgets. The mid-size seller — someone running $50K–$5M a year across multiple channels — had been completely abandoned.

Syndrax Sync exists to fix that. A platform powerful enough for serious operations, accessible enough for one-person shops, and autonomous enough to actually reduce your workload instead of adding to it.

Values

What We Believe

Build for Sellers

We obsess over seller outcomes. Every feature is measured by one question: does this save time, increase profit, or prevent losses? If the answer is no, we don't build it.

Transparency First

Your data is yours. Your business is yours. We provide tools and intelligence — you drive the results. Full visibility, open API, no lock-in.

Trust Through Automation

Our agents work 24/7 without mistakes, but you're always in control. They follow your rules. You make the calls. Automation should amplify you, not replace your judgment.
