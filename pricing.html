Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free tier?

The Starter plan is completely free and includes access to 3 marketplaces, 100 product listings, 1 limited agent, the profitability dashboard, and community support. No credit card required.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately on upgrade, and at the end of your billing cycle on downgrade.

Do you offer annual pricing discounts?

Yes — annual plans receive a 20% discount on Growth and Scale tiers. Contact us to set up annual billing.

Is there a setup fee?

None. There are no setup fees, onboarding fees, or hidden charges on any plan. What you see is what you pay.

What about enterprise pricing?

Custom pricing is available for teams with specific volume, compliance, or integration requirements. Contact our enterprise team to discuss.

Do you offer custom pricing for high-volume sellers?

Yes. Sellers with 50,000+ listings or custom workflow needs can work directly with our team on a tailored package.
